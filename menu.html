Pasta

Carbonara

A creamy sauce made with eggs, cheese , pancetta or guanciale, black pepper, and sometimes cream.


Bolognese

A rich and meaty sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, carrots, celery, red wine, and herbs.


Pesto

A spicy sauce made with tomatoes, garlic, chili peppers, and olive oil, adding a fiery kick to the pasta.


Amatriciana

A sauce made with tomatoes, guanciale, onions, pecorino cheese, and red pepper flakes, providing a bold and savory flavor.


Aglio e Olio

A simple yet flavorful sauce made with garlic, olive oil, and red pepper flakes, often accompanied by parsley and grated cheese.


Alfredo

A creamy sauce made with butter, Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream, creating a rich and indulgent flavor.

A sauce made with tomatoes, olives, capers, anchovies, garlic, and herbs, offering a salty, tangy, and slightly briny taste.


Vongole

A sauce made with clams, garlic, white wine, parsley, and olive oil, resulting in a delicate and seafood-infused flavor.
